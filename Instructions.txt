OBJECTIVE
------------------------------------------------------------
Get the TROPHY before time runs out
to advance to the next level.


BEFORE YOU PLAY
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Register or log in according to the game menu.
2. Choose your character by typing the number of the option.


BASIC RULES
------------------------------------------------------------
- You always start at the ENTRANCE of the maze.
- You CANNOT go through walls.
- If you hit a wall, look for another path.
- Time is limited: use it wisely.
